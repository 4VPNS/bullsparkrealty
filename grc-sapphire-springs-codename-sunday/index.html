About The Project

Discover your dream home at GRC Sapphire Spring, an exquisite residential development nestled in Gundappa Layout, Gottigere, Bangalore. Offering a range of 2, 2.5, 3, and 4 BHK apartments, this pre-launch project combines contemporary design with world-class amenities.

With spacious layouts ranging from 800 to 1700 sq. ft. and a towering G+13 structure, every home promises comfort and elegance. Spread across 10 acres, GRC Sapphire Spring features meticulously planned units, ensuring ample open spaces and premium facilities.
