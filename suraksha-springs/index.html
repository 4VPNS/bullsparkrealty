About Suraksha Springs

Discover luxury living in Bangalore

Suraksha Springs is an upcoming residential project on Begur Road, Bangalore, offering 2 & 3 BHK apartments that spreads across 2.25 acres. This G+4 structure comprises 180 units, designed to cater to modern family needs.

The 2 BHK apartments range from 1040-1195 sqft, and the 3 BHK apartments range from 1385-1515 sqft. Possession begins in January 2026.

Strategically located, Suraksha Springs ensures connectivity to essential amenities, educational institutions, and entertainment hubs. This pre-launch venture promises comfort, elegance, and innovation, setting a new standard in contemporary living in Bangalore.
